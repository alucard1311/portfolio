About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm a passionate robotics engineer currently pursuing my Master's in Computer Engineering at Virginia Tech with a 3.83 GPA. My expertise spans autonomous underwater vehicles (AUVs), multi-robot systems, and advanced perception algorithms.

Currently working as a Graduate Research Assistant at Virginia Tech's Center for Marine Autonomy and Robotics, I lead the development of the Dragon AUV for acoustic tracking of marine targets. My work focuses on cutting-edge underwater robotics, decentralized multi-agent systems, and robust perception in challenging environments.

With professional experience at Aerospace Engineers and IntRobotics, I've developed navigation algorithms, sensor fusion systems, and perception modules that enable robots to operate autonomously in complex, real-world scenarios.
